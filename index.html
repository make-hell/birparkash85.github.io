OUR PRODUCTS

CAP

As a wholesale manufacturer, we provide a complete selection of pipe caps, including threaded and plain options, in various materials. Our caps ensure a leak-proof seal and provide reliable closure for all your industrial and plumbing projects.

NIPPLE

We are a leading producer of pipe nipples, offering a variety of sizes, configurations, and materials for industrial and residential use. Our nipples are rigorously tested to provide a durable, high-strength connection for seamless piping systems.

NUT

We manufacture premium, high-tensile nuts from our own quality forgings, ensuring superior strength and performance in demanding applications. Available in a wide range of sizes and materials to meet all your wholesale industrial needs.

Bend Pipe

We fabricate a wide array of high-quality bend pipes, available in various bend angles, radii, and materials to change pipeline direction. Our bend pipes are made for seamless integration and optimal flow in all industrial applications.

Forging

We produce high-strength industrial forgings, the foundational raw material for our entire product line, available in various steel grades. Our forgings feature an enhanced grain structure and superior mechanical properties for maximum durability and reliability.
